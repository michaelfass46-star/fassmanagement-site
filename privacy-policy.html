Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 1, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Fass Management ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and handles information in connection with our internal analytics tools and applications, including our use of third-party platform APIs.

1. Who We Are

Fass Management is an investment research firm that develops and operates internal data pipelines and analytics tools to support portfolio research and decision-making. Our tools aggregate publicly available trend and market data from digital platforms for internal analytical purposes.

2. Information We Access

Our applications access the following types of data via authorized platform APIs (such as the Pinterest API):

  • Trend and keyword data: Aggregated, anonymized search volume and interest trend data for specified keywords, brands, and product categories.
  • Public content metrics: Publicly available engagement and interest signals at the aggregate level.

We do not access, collect, or store any personally identifiable information (PII) about individual platform users. All data accessed is aggregated and anonymized at the source.

3. How We Use This Information

Data accessed through platform APIs is used solely for internal investment research purposes, specifically:

  • Monitoring consumer interest trends across brands, products, and categories over time.
  • Comparing relative brand and product engagement to identify consumer behavior shifts.
  • Informing internal portfolio research and analysis.

We do not use this data for advertising, marketing, or any commercial purpose beyond internal research.

4. Data Storage and Security

Trend data retrieved via APIs is stored in internal, access-controlled databases used exclusively by Fass Management personnel. We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect stored data from unauthorized access or disclosure.

We do not sell, license, share, or distribute any data obtained through platform APIs to third parties.

5. Third-Party APIs

Our tools interact with third-party platform APIs (including the Pinterest API) in compliance with each platform's Terms of Service and Developer Policies. We only request the minimum permissions necessary for our research use case. Our use of data obtained through these APIs is governed by the respective platform's terms in addition to this policy.

6. Data Retention

Aggregated trend data is retained for as long as it is useful for research purposes. Because all data is anonymized and aggregated, there is no personally identifiable information to delete.

7. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal information about individual users, there is no personal data subject to access, correction, or deletion requests. If you have questions about our data practices, please contact us at the address below.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
